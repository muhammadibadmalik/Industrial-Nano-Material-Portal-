Construction Industry

NanoCal plays a critical role in modern construction, particularly in concrete applications where time and performance are crucial factors.

Our nano-activated calcium chloride accelerates cement hydration, enabling faster setting times and earlier strength development. This is especially valuable in cold weather construction where traditional concrete would take significantly longer to cure.

✓
Accelerates concrete setting time by up to 50%
✓
Enables construction in temperatures below 0°C
✓
Improves early-age compressive strength
✓
Reduces formwork removal time

Road Maintenance & De-Icing

Keep roads, highways, and critical infrastructure safe during winter months with our advanced de-icing solutions.

NanoCal de-icing grade provides superior ice melting performance at lower temperatures compared to traditional rock salt. The nano-scale particles create a faster brine solution, breaking the bond between ice and pavement more effectively.

Effective at temperatures down to -30°C
