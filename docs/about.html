Our Story

NanoCal Industries was founded in 1999 with a vision to revolutionize the industrial chemicals sector through advanced nano-technology. What began as a small research facility has grown into a global leader in nano-activated calcium chloride production.

Our proprietary nano-activation process, developed through years of intensive research, produces calcium chloride particles at the nanometer scale. This breakthrough technology delivers superior performance across all applications, from moisture absorption to ice melting.

Today, we serve over 500 industrial clients across 50+ countries, providing innovative solutions that improve efficiency, reduce costs, and enable new possibilities in diverse industries.
