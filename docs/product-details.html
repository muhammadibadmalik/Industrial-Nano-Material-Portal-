Product Description

NanoCal Desiccant Grade is engineered using our proprietary nano-activation process, resulting in calcium chloride particles at the nanometer scale. This advanced processing technique dramatically increases the surface area available for moisture absorption, making it significantly more effective than traditional desiccants.

Key Benefits:

  • 300% increased surface area compared to conventional CaCl₂
  • Faster moisture absorption rate
  • Higher total moisture capacity
  • Longer effective lifespan
  • Food-safe and FDA compliant
